Lil Bro's Adventure Back Home

Lil Bro has been on a long journey. It is time for Lil Bro to get back home, and he needs your help.
Help Lil Bro navigate through mountains, caves and different terrains to find his way back home.



About the game

This game is a semester project that was created by team The Googlers
We started off by wanting to create a 2d platform game.
Then we expanded on the environment of the game and concluded that the story of our main character searching for its way back home would be a cool idea.
Hence, why it is called Lil Bro's adventure back home.

Visuals and screenshots from the game

Gameplay details and features

The game is a simple 2d platformer in which you navigate through different terrains to reach the door to Lil Bro's house

Though you have to be careful since there are traps such as spikes and sawblades on the road back home

Assisting Lil Bro are different powerups such as jump boost and speed boost which will help the player navigate through the traps much easier

Controls

  • 'W': Jump
  • 'A': Left
  • 'S': Down
  • 'D': Right
  • 'Space': Use Item Lil Bro has in his inventory
  • 'P': Start/Pause
  • 'R': Restart game
  • 'H': Switch to infoScreen
  • 'B': Switch to previous screen
  • 'esc': Exit program

Final words

The game is finished, and you have the opportunity to play it by downloading the files here: Lil bro's adventure back home

This game is suitable for all ages and anyone can have fun playing this game
